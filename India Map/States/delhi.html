Welcome to Delhi

About Delhi

Delhi, the capital of India, is a bustling metropolis that combines ancient history with modern advancements. Known for its rich heritage, vibrant culture, and political significance, Delhi is a city that offers a blend of tradition and modernity.

Geography

Situated in northern India, Delhi is bordered by Haryana on three sides and Uttar Pradesh to the east. The city is located along the banks of the Yamuna River and is divided into Old Delhi and New Delhi.

Tourist Attractions

  • Red Fort: A UNESCO World Heritage Site and a symbol of India's rich history
  • India Gate: A war memorial and iconic landmark
  • Qutub Minar: An ancient tower and UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • Lotus Temple: A beautiful Bahá'í House of Worship
  • Akshardham Temple: Known for its stunning architecture

Culture

Delhi is a melting pot of cultures, where people from all parts of the country coexist. The city's festivals, such as Diwali, Holi, Eid, and Christmas, are celebrated with great enthusiasm. Delhi's cuisine, from street food like chaat and parathas to fine dining, is a treat for food lovers.
