Geography

Assam is a state in northeast India with a tropical monsoon rainforest climate. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, fertile valleys, and mighty rivers.

Tourist Attractions

  • Guwahati: Guwahati will steal your heart with its magnificent ancient temples, stunning hills, diverse wildlife and sprawling tea plantations.
  • Kaziranga National Park: It is famous as the abode of two-thirds of the world's great one-horned rhinoceroses.
  • Dibrugarh: Dibrugarh, nestled in Assam, is a picturesque city renowned for its sprawling tea gardens and vibrant cultural heritage.
  • Silchar: The city of Silchar, or Shilchar as it is pronounced locally, is located on the banks of the Barak River.

Culture

Assam's culture is a rich tapestry of various cultures, religions, and traditions. The state is home to many tribes, each with its own distinct culture, language, and lifestyle.
