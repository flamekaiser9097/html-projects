Geography

It is situated on the west coast of India. The state is bounded by the Arabian Sea on the west, Pakistan and Rajasthan in the north and north-east respectively, Madhya Pradesh in the south-east and Maharashtra in the South. The state has an area of 1,96,024 sq. km., the seventh largest state in terms of area in India.

Tourist Attractions

  • Ahmedabad: Ahmedabad is home to the famous Sabarmati Ashram and will charm you with its iconic mosques, tombs, museums and vibrant bazaars.
  • Dwarka: It is an important pilgrimage centre for Hindus from all over the world.
  • Kutch: Flaunting surreal stretches of sparkling white land, diverse wildlife, pristine seashores and historical sites, Kutch will leave you spellbound with its vibrant culture and exotic charm.
  • Sasan Gir: Home of the Asiatic Lion, Sasan Gir is famous for the Gir National Park with abundant flora, fauna and birdlife.
  • Vadodara: it is known for its architecture, classical music and financial superiority.

Culture

The culture of Gujarat is a vibrant and it is famousfor its true colors of rich history and traditions. The state is a blend of many religions like Hinduism, Islam, Jainism and Buddhism. And Gujarati culture blends in arts, beliefs, customs, languages, technology and values.
