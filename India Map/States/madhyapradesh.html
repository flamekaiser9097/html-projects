About Madhya Pradesh

Madhya Pradesh, located in the heart of India, is known for its rich history, magnificent temples, and diverse wildlife. The state is often referred to as the "Heart of India" due to its central location.

Geography

Madhya Pradesh shares its borders with Uttar Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Maharashtra, Gujarat, and Rajasthan. The state capital, Bhopal, is known for its beautiful lakes and greenery. Indore, the largest city, is a major industrial hub.

Culture

Madhya Pradesh boasts a rich cultural heritage, reflected in its music, dance, and art. The state is famous for folk music and traditional dances like Ghoomar and Rai. Handicrafts such as Chanderi and Maheshwari sarees are highly prized. Festivals like Navratri, Diwali, and Tansen Music Festival are celebrated with great enthusiasm.
